Xatworld (Powered by SHOUTcast™ radio) IP search provides Xat users a free and easy way to both find and add radio stations and their corresponding IP's to any Xat chatbox or radio power.

You can search by radio station, keyword, artist, genre, and much more. Each radio station can also be previewed via a TUNE IN link. When you've found one that interests you, you simply click the GET IP button and you are presented with that radio stations IP address. Use that IP to add a radio station to your Xat or radio power.

What is SHOUTcast?
SHOUTcast™ Radio is one of the largest directories of professionally and community programmed online radio stations in the world. Today SHOUTcast™ Radio features over 25,000 stations from around the globe.
